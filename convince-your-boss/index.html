You're staring at your screen right now. You might be at work, you might be lounging on your bed, you might be on the subway.

You are staring at your screen right now because you love design.

You live, breathe, and eat design and think the whole world should, too. Since you're staring at your screen, you're probably not talking with other designers at the moment; you probably haven't spoken to a designer outside of work in weeks. Let's do something about that.

It's time to unleash your creativity and your ideas. Meet and interact with 1000 designers in the flesh at UI/UX Conf in Shanghai this October. Learn about new trends, find people that agree with and that challenge your design philosophies, make lifelong friends that are also making the world a more well-designed place.

This two-day conference will have networking, talks by 20 top designers from all over the world, and workshops for you to grow personally and professionally as a designer.

UI/UX Conf China was born from Wiredcraft's monthly UIUX meetups in Shanghai, which took off like wildfire. The meetups soon had waitlists longer than your arm and people were clamoring for more opportunities to hang out and network, so the folks at Wiredcraft put together this conference to unite all of these interested designers and design experts from around the world.

Now the tricky part: covering your ticket and other expenses

Good news, you can convince your boss. Going to conferences isn't just about individual growth; it can really help your team. Benefits for your company for attending UI/UX Conf:

  1. You can find awesome designers, like yourself, to hire full-time or freelance
  2. You will learn new design skills/techniques/inspiration during our talks
  3. You'll be refreshed, motivated, and ready to get back to work and do great designing
  4. Your company's name recognition and brand will be more widely known, thanks to this connected global design community
  5. You'll have great stories to share with your team for inspiration and brainstorming
  6. You can find new business leads for your company to pursue
  7. You'll probably come back happier, healthier, less stressed, and more productive
